Summer Upon Us

Summer's just about here and I'm not nearly as far done with my project as I was hoping. I'll be taking some much needed rest from work to wrench away on my project and hopefully, with any luck, get it done. I noticed by some of the posts on the forums that we have some new people venturing into this swap and I hope that they find their way here to help us out! 

We are needing writeups in the following areas to help complete the information store: 

Engine Mounting

Throttle Cable Connections

Wiring (anything outside of what has been posted or possibly for more clarity)

Transmissions

Parts Sources (with item number, description, and price)

and anything else that you can think of that hasn't been posted yet. 

What we hope this site will bring is a COMPLETE information store for this swap. I WILL NOT pull ANY information of any sort from any other website without express written permission of the originating author. I'm basically saying that if it isn't your's, don't submit it. Only original writeups please. We don't want to tick anyone off, just want to help people out in providing as accurate and complete information as we can. If the information has been pulled from another site, it is annotated as such and proper credit is always given to the author. Please submit all articles, pics, etc... to the email link in the navigation bar to the left. 

Thanks!

Bob AKA 85Frankenstein
